About Cricket
History of Cricket in 10 Points

1. Cricket originated in England and has a history dating back to the 16th century.

2. The sport evolved from rural games played with a ball and stick.

3. The Marylebone Cricket Club (MCC) was formed in the 18th century, establishing standardized rules.

4. International cricket began with the first recognized Test match in 1877 between England and Australia.

5. Limited-overs cricket, or One Day Internationals (ODIs), was introduced in the 1960s.

6. The shorter and more dynamic Twenty20 (T20) format gained popularity in the 21st century.

7. Cricket expanded beyond England to other countries, particularly through the British Empire.

8. The sport has seen the rise of domestic leagues like the Indian Premier League (IPL).

9. The International Cricket Council (ICC) serves as the governing body for cricket worldwide.

10. Cricket continues to captivate global audiences with its strategic gameplay and rich traditions.
